About Maizena

Maizena has developed the world's first liquid feedstock for 3D printing. We produce 3D printing systems (feeds, printer heads, reservoirs, etc.) using cornstarch material.

Maizena 3D

Will offer custom feedstock, printheads, product supply vessels, and software solutions for hobbyists and professionals.

Maizena Services

Wil take on custom prototyping assignments using Maizena technologies for rapid, sustainable, small, and large-scale 3D printed objects.

Maizena Packaging

Will provide customized biobased and biodegradable packaging for products required special case in shipping, including art objects, and other high value products.
