Why Corn Starch?

Corn starch is a cheap, biodegradable material that cna be used as an alternative to ABS plastic for 3D printer feed input.

A box of corn starch at the store is about $1.50. Or about $500 per metric ton.

It is easy to clean up (just add water!)

It is biodegradable.

Output can be flexible and expandible.

In the right regime, the printed material expands when cooled.

Applications

  • Print flexible molds.
  • Print your own Cheetos!
  • Print packing material for complex structures.
