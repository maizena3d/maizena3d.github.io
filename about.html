About Maizena3D

Maizena 3D has developed the world's first liquid feedstock for 3D printing. We produce 3D printing systems (feeds, printer heads, resevoirs, etc.) using corn starch material. There are two types of feedstock - our sister companies, Maizena Alpha and Maizena Beta, produce these.

Maizena Alpha

Maizena Alpha produces a biodegradable polymer from liquid feedstock for sustainable cost effective 3D printing.

Maizena Beta

Maizena Beta produces an expanded polymer that will, for the first time, enable large-scale affordable 3D printing for prototyping at scale and a myriad of other applications
